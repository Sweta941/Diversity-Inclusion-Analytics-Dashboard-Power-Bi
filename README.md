📌 Project Overview:

This project is a 3-page interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze workforce diversity and inclusion metrics for a pharmaceutical company (Pharma Group AG) across FY20 and FY21. 
The goal is to help HR stakeholders identify gender equity gaps across hiring, promotions, performance, and executive representation — and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Problem Statement:

Organizations often struggle to measure and communicate diversity progress clearly. This dashboard addresses:

(i) Is gender representation improving across job levels over time?
(ii) Are male and female employees being promoted and rated equally?
(iii) Where are the biggest equity gaps — by department, region, or age group?

📂 Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 — Gender by Job Level

- Headcount and % female/male split by job level (FY20 vs FY21).
- Time-in-role by gender across job levels.
- FY20 attrition trends by performance rating and gender.
- Combo charts showing employee count alongside % female representation.

### Page 2 — Performance & Executive Pipeline

- Gender performance rating distribution (FY20)
- Executive split: FY20 vs FY21 comparison
- Executive hires and promotions-to-executive breakdown
- Average performance rating KPIs by gender
- Employee distribution by age group and job level

### Page 3 — Workforce Overview

- KPI cards: Total employees, male count, female count, FY20 promotions
- Promotion rates for FY20 and FY21
- Employee breakdown by nationality and geographic region
- Gender split by region

🔍 Key Insights

- Female representation decreases significantly at senior and executive job levels
- Male and female average performance ratings are comparable, yet promotion rates differ
- Majority of executive hires in FY20 skewed heavily male
- Certain departments and regions show stronger gender balance than others
